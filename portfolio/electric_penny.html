Electric Penny Board (Ongoing Project)

DIY Electric Penny Board with Wireless Remote

Currently working on an electric penny board which I hope to complete in time for the nicer weather. Planning on using the strong neodynimum magnets in the photo above to attach the battery to the bottom of the board. Will be 3D printing most parts/cases for the electronics/battery; using Bluetooth modules (HC-05) to transfer data about battery levels and desired speed.


Motor attached


Remote contains Bluetooth module, lipo charging board, and microcontroller


Features slider potentiometer and enable trigger button


Lipo charger module ensures safe recharging of battery through micro USB

On hold for now

On top of mounting the motor, I got as far as assembling and getting the Bluetooth remote to work and calibrate the ESC. I was able to drive the skateboard, bu unfortunately the 3D printed plastic started to flex too much under pressure, and finally broke. The motor attachment needs to be redesigned to withstand more force, likely using something other than 3D printed PLA plastic. For now, this project is on hold until I have some more time.
