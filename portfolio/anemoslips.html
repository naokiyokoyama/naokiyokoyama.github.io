AnemoSlips

InnoService Competition

My university's Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) chapter participated in SASE's national InnoService Competition 2015 (again). This time, we were prompted to "design a consumer product for the digitally-connected consumer," and came up with AnemoSlips. AnemoSlips is a portable and disposable microfiber pouch that sanitizes both your hands and phones, protecting the user from preventable illness. This time I was one of two leaders for our team of six. I came up with the initial product concept, planned out the theoretical timeline for our product, and filmed/edited the video, in additon to checking in and making sure our team was on top of things. We won 3rd place for $1000 (again!)!
